How It Works

  • Paste your Spotify playlist link into the input box on the home page.
  • Click the "Download MP3" button.
  • The system fetches the song names and artists from your playlist.
  • It searches YouTube for the best match and downloads the audio in MP3 format.
  • All songs are zipped into one file and made available for download.
  • Just save the downloaded ZIP and extract your songs.
